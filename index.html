Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are the parades free to watch?
A: Macy's Parade, Chicago Lights Festival, and Hollywood Christmas Parade are completely FREE - just find a spot along the route! Disney parades require park admission tickets.
Q: How early should I arrive for a good viewing spot?
A:
  • üî¥ Prime front-row spots: 4-6 hours early
  • üü° Good viewing: 2-3 hours early
  • üü¢ Any spot (further back): 1 hour early
Q: What can I bring and what's prohibited?
‚úÖ ALLOWED:
  • Small folding chairs
  • Food and non-alcoholic beverages
  • Cameras and phones
  • Blankets
‚ùå PROHIBITED:
  • Large backpacks
  • Ladders
  • Selfie sticks (at most parades)
  • Alcohol
  • Pets (except service animals)
Q: Will the parade be canceled if it rains or snows?
A: Parades proceed rain or shine! Only extreme weather conditions (hurricanes, severe storms) will cause cancellation. Come prepared with rain gear and waterproof clothing.
Q: How long do the parades last?
A:
  • Macy's Parade: 3 hours
  • Disney Parades: 20-30 minutes
  • Chicago Festival: ~2 hours
  • Hollywood Parade: 2-3 hours
Q: When's the best time to book flights and hotels?
A: Book 2-3 months in advance for best rates. Avoid booking on Thanksgiving Day itself (most expensive). Use flight comparison sites and set price alerts. Consider red-eye flights for cheaper fares.
Q: Is it family-friendly? What age is best for kids?
A:
  • Ages 3+: Can remember the experience
  • Ages 5+: Can stand/wait comfortably
  • Ages 8+: Ideal age to fully enjoy
Family Tips:
  • Bring stroller (doubles as gear carrier)
  • Pack favorite snacks
  • Download movies/games for wait time
  • Use child ID tags with contact info
